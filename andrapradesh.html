Festivals in Andhra Pradesh

Ugadi

Ugadi marks the Telugu New Year and is celebrated with traditional foods, decorations, and cultural events.

Sankranti

Sankranti is a harvest festival celebrated with kite flying, bonfires, and delicious sweets.

Tirupati Brahmotsavam

The Tirupati Brahmotsavam is a grand religious festival featuring processions and rituals at the Tirumala temple.
