Festivals in Karnataka

Dasara

Dasara, also known as Navaratri, is the state festival of Karnataka, celebrated with grandeur in Mysore. The Mysore Palace is beautifully lit, and a royal procession, cultural events, and performances take place over ten days.

Karaga

The Karaga Festival is a unique celebration in Bangalore, dedicated to the goddess Draupadi. It involves a midnight procession, traditional dance, and rituals performed by the Vahnikula Kshatriya community.

Hampi Utsav

Hampi Utsav, or Vijaya Utsav, is a vibrant cultural festival held in the historic city of Hampi. The event features music, dance performances, puppet shows, and processions, celebrating Karnatakaâ€™s rich heritage.

Ugadi

Ugadi marks the New Year for Kannadigas and is celebrated with traditional food, decorations, and family gatherings. It is a time of joy, where people exchange sweets and seek blessings for prosperity.
